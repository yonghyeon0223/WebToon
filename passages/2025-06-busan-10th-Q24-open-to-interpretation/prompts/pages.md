═══════════════════════════════════════════

must look. Reproduce them EXACTLY: same colors, proportions, details,
architectural features. Do NOT reinterpret, redesign, or "improve" them. This
page composes EXISTING designs into a new scene.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
STYLE — PIXAR × MR. MEN HYBRID
═══════════════════════════════════════════
Render as if PIXAR published a Roger Hargreaves Mr. Men picture book —
illustrated medium with PIXAR craft (NOT a 3D animation still).

PRESERVE Mr. Men identity:
- Iconic geometric body silhouettes (round / rectangle / oval)
- Stick-thin arms ending in white mitten hands, stubby legs with simple shoes
- Big simple expressive faces with dot eyes
- Bold black character outlines (soft hand-drawn feel, NOT razor-sharp 3D)
- Signature character props (kept exactly as shown in references)

ADD PIXAR craft:
- Subtle volumetric shading on bodies (soft form light + gentle underside)
- Soft ambient occlusion in crevices
- Real-feeling materials (brass, leather, fabric, wood, glass — as shown in refs)
- Warm cinematic lighting (PIXAR's signature golden warmth)
- Polished animation studio finish — designed and intentional

AVOID: pure flat 2D (crude), pure 3D (loses Mr. Men identity), razor-sharp
CGI edges, photorealistic clutter, tacked-on motif icons.

═══════════════════════════════════════════

═══════════════════════════════════════════
Render Korean text on the page using:

NARRATION → soft cream rectangular plate with hand-painted imperfect edges

DIALOGUE → classic round speech bubble with hand-drawn imperfect outline

Character-thematic colors for English vocabulary words (slightly bolder):
