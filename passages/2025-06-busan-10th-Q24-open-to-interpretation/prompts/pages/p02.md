# P02 — 하늘이 무너졌다는 패닉

## Refs
- **First** (p01) — anchor: Mr. Realistic's likeness and the front-gate / house setting (he's a calm anchor at frame edge). Mr. Worried is a new cameo — design fresh, distinct silhouette and color, don't inherit Mr. Realistic's body shape.

## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1. Standing at his own front door / inside his gate, facing OUTWARD toward the visitor. Calm, attentive, host-like posture (this is HIS home; the visitor came to him).
- **Mr. Worried** (cameo, debuts here): small round bright-yellow Mr. Men body, both stick-arms thrown up dramatically toward the sky, panicked frowning open mouth, leaning back as if recoiling. Distinctly different silhouette from any main character.

## Action
Spatial composition matters: **Mr. Realistic stands at the threshold of his own house** (at the door or just inside the open gate, framed by his cottage behind him), receiving a visitor. **Mr. Worried has come to him from the village**, standing OUTSIDE the gate or on the path approaching the house, arms thrown up in panic mid-yelp.

Camera angle: a 3/4 view that clearly shows (a) Mr. Realistic's house behind Mr. Realistic, anchoring him as the homeowner, and (b) Mr. Worried standing on the outside / village side, clearly a visitor arriving — NOT someone coming out of the house. Mr. Worried's body angled TOWARD Mr. Realistic. Mr. Realistic's body angled outward toward Mr. Worried, calm and listening.

Mr. Worried's panic is the focal expression of the page, but Mr. Realistic must be clearly present and clearly the host.

## Background
Mr. Realistic's house is centered/behind Mr. Realistic — terracotta door, magnifying-glass knocker, porthole window visible (same as Page 1). The gate / front path is in view. Same warm late-afternoon golden hour as Page 1. **Important: the sky above is perfectly calm and normal** — soft pale blue with a few stylized clouds. The contrast between Mr. Worried's panic and the calm sky IS the visual joke.
