# P02 — 하늘이 무너졌다는 패닉

## Character
- **Mr. Worried** (cameo, debuts here): small round bright-yellow Mr. Men body, both stick-arms thrown up dramatically toward the sky, panicked frowning open mouth, leaning back as if recoiling. Distinctly different silhouette from any main character.
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1. Visible only as a hint at the edge of frame (his shoulder or arm, or the back of his head) — establishes that Mr. Worried came TO him, but he's not the focus this page.

## Action
Mr. Worried in the foreground at Mr. Realistic's front gate, both arms shot up toward the sky in panic, body recoiling backward, mouth open in a yelp. Mr. Realistic appears partially at the edge of frame as a calm anchor — just enough presence to set up Page 3, but Mr. Worried's panic is the focus.

## Background
Same gate-front setting as Page 1, same warm late-afternoon golden hour. **Important: the sky above is perfectly calm and normal** — soft pale blue with a few stylized clouds. The contrast between Mr. Worried's panic and the calm sky IS the visual joke.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "누가 와서 울먹여요." — plain sepia.
- Speech bubble (Mr. Worried, jagged shaky lettering, tail to his mouth): "하늘이 무너지는 것 같아요!" — plain sepia (cameo, no color emphasis).
