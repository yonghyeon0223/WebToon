# P18 — 셋의 자신감 (vocab: knowledge)

## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1.
- **Mr. Logical** — same as Page 6.
- **Mr. Rational** — same as Page 12.

## Action
A hero team-shot of the three friends together in Mr. Realistic's living room. Each engaged with their signature object — Mr. Realistic peering through his magnifying glass at an open book on the coffee table; Mr. Logical at his slate, chalking another stepwise reasoning; Mr. Rational with his notebook open, pencil mid-stroke. Confident comfortable poses, golden warm light. Books, scrolls, and stacked papers around them — visualizing the "knowledge" the narration describes.

## Background
**Mr. Realistic's living room** (debuts here): warm wood floors, dusty pink sofa against the back wall, round wooden coffee table in the foreground with three teacups, tall window letting in warm late-afternoon light, a tall bookshelf packed with books on one side, a beige armchair near the window. Cozy lived-in feel.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "이 셋이 모이면 못 푸는 일이 없대요. 세 사람의 머릿속엔 책에서 읽은 것, 직접 겪은 것, 사람들에게 들은 것 — 그러니까 풍부한 **knowledge**가 가득했거든요." — paint the English word "knowledge" in a soft warm amber-gold (a shared trait of all three, no single character owner). Korean plain sepia.

## Vocab tag

## Note
This is the page that visually establishes the three friends as a team AND introduces the living room (which Chapter 2 will rely on heavily). Slightly longer narration; consider stacking across two narration plates if needed. Compose with the bottom-right corner clear for the vocab parchment.
