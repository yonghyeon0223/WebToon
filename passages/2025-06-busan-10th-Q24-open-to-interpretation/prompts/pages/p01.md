# P01 — Mr. Realistic 등장

## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** (main character — establishing reference for the whole book):
  - **Body silhouette**: a near-perfect round body — almost a sphere with a flat bottom where the legs join. Medium height (~1.5× as tall as wide), slightly bottom-heavy / squat / grounded. NOT egg-shaped, NOT oval — a true round body that reads as stable and centered.
  - **Color**: warm earthy terracotta-orange, like sun-baked clay pottery. Subtle slightly-darker gradient toward the lower half (suggests groundedness in earth).
  - **Face features**: small round dot eyes, evenly spaced, centered; framed by round black-rimmed eyeglasses sitting at eye level (the lenses are wider than the dot eyes themselves). NO eyebrows. Mouth is a soft small upward-curve closed-mouth smile (calm, not wide). Subtle pink blush mark on each cheek for warmth. No head adornment, no hair.
  - **Signature prop**: brass-handled magnifying glass on a brown leather cord hanging around his neck; the glass itself rests at chest level. Brass has a soft metallic sheen, the lens has a faint catchlight.
  - **Posture & stance**: wide planted stance with brown leather shoes on small stubby legs; weight evenly distributed; body upright, never tilted. Mittens at relaxed sides or one casually touching a nearby object. Reads as grounded, observational, welcoming.
  - **Default expression**: calm confident closed-mouth smile; observational; reads "I see you and I'm listening."

## Action
Standing relaxed at the front gate of his house, one mitten lightly resting on the gate latch as if he just opened it; body slightly turned outward toward the viewer. A "this is my home, welcome" pose.

## Background
**Mr. Realistic's house** (front view): warm beige cottage with terracotta door, brass magnifying-glass-shaped door knocker, circular porthole "lens" window, a small hanging illustrated sign above the door. Late golden hour, warm sun bathing the facade. Hint of village rooftops softly out-of-focus in the distance.

## Text
- Narration plate (top, cream parchment): "이 동네에서 가장 유명한 사람은 Mr. Realistic이에요." — paint "Mr. Realistic" in his terracotta-orange.
