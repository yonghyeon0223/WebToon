## Character
- **Mr. Logical** (main character — establishing reference for the whole book; must look distinctly different from Mr. Realistic from silhouette alone):
  - **Body silhouette**: tall slim vertical rectangle with rounded corners. Height ≈ 2.5× his width — clearly the tallest of the three friends. Top-heavy: head/cap area takes up the upper third of his profile. NOT round (vs Mr. Realistic), NOT square — a clear standing rectangle. Reads as upright, formal, "scholar at attention."
  - **Color**: deep cool cobalt-blue, solid (no gradient). Cooler tone than Mr. Realistic's warm terracotta — the temperature difference alone makes them distinct.
  - **Face features**: small round dot eyes, slightly smaller and closer-together than Mr. Realistic's. **Has eyebrows** (Realistic has none) — thin black slightly-arched eyebrows that signal alertness / confidence. Mouth is a small measured upward-curve closed-mouth smile (more clipped than Realistic's warm curve). NO blush — a cooler, more composed face. **Mortarboard graduation cap** sits squarely on top of his head, with a small gold tassel hanging from the right corner.
  - **Signature prop**: a small slate chalkboard with a wooden frame, held at chest level or tucked under one arm. White chalk in the other mitten. The slate often shows "1.   2.   3.   ∴" written in confident white chalk strokes.
  - **Posture & stance**: upright measured stance with slightly forward weight; head often tilted slightly up confidently. Mittens often gesture as he counts off points (one mitten extended, the other holding the slate). Reads as confident, didactic, never slouched.
  - **Default expression**: small confident closed-mouth smile; measured; reads "I have it figured out."
