# P06 — Mr. Logical 첫 등장

## Character
- **Mr. Logical** (debuts here): tall slim cobalt-blue rectangular Mr. Men body, mortarboard graduation cap with a small tassel, holding a small slate chalkboard tucked under one arm or held at chest level. White chalk in his other mitten. Confident measured expression, small friendly closed-mouth smile.

## Action
A character-introduction shot. Mr. Logical stands chest-up to half-body framed, slightly turned toward the viewer in a friendly intro pose — perhaps a small tip of the mortarboard with one mitten, or a "nice to meet you" gesture. His slate visible, scribbled with faint chalk marks "1. 2. 3. ∴".

## Background
Warm neutral cream-parchment background with soft-focus scholarly motifs — a couple of book spines, a faint chalkboard, ink pots — all blurred and low-contrast so they read as "his world" without committing to a specific location.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "Mr. Realistic에겐 절친이 둘 있어요. 첫 번째 친구는 Mr. Logical이에요." — paint "Mr. Realistic" in his terracotta-orange and "Mr. Logical" in his cobalt-blue.
