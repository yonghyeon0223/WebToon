## Prompt — Gemini에 그대로 복붙

```
PICTURE BOOK PAGE — AS IF PIXAR PUBLISHED A MR. MEN PICTURE BOOK.

Aspect ratio 9:16 vertical (mobile picture-book format).

═══════════════════════════════════════════
STYLE — "PIXAR PUBLISHED A MR. MEN PICTURE BOOK"
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Painterly illustrated picture book medium with PIXAR sensibility — NOT 3D animation still. Materials suggested through painted texture and color. Intentional warm color grading. Composition designed like a published storybook page. Hand-drawn confident outlines (soft, charming — NOT 3D edges).

Use the attached references — Mr. Realistic (mr_realistic.png) and his house exterior (realistic_house.png). The worried character is anonymous and described in the prompt.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE — PIXAR COMIC TIMING
═══════════════════════════════════════════

In front of Mr. Realistic's house. Two characters with EXAGGERATED OPPOSITE BODY LANGUAGE — a PIXAR-style physical comedy beat where the reader instantly reads the contrast:

LEFT (worried character — anonymous "Mr. Worried" type):
- Small round bright-yellow Mr. Men body, painterly rendered in PIXAR × Mr. Men style consistent with the rest of the book
- Body LEANING BACKWARD as if recoiling from the imagined falling sky — feet barely on the ground, almost stumbling
- Both stick arms thrown up dramatically toward the sky
- Wide-open frowning mouth in genuine panic, eyes squeezed in distress
- Tiny droplets of cartoon-style sweat or worry-lines around his head (painterly, charming — not crude comic effects)

RIGHT (Mr. Realistic, use reference):
- Standing relaxed and grounded, magnifying glass held casually at his side
- ONE MITTEN HAND TUCKED CASUALLY BEHIND HIS BACK in a "this is fine" pose
- Head tilted slightly upward looking at the sky with calm composed examining expression
- Subtle little smile — observing rather than reacting

The visual contrast IS the comedy — even before reading the text, the body language tells the joke.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
COMPOSITION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

- Two-shot, both characters in the lower portion of the frame
- Slight worm's-eye-view angle (sky dominates upper two-thirds)
- The sky is calm and normal — soft pale-blue with painterly cloud shapes (NOT actually falling — that's the joke)
- Mr. Realistic's house in the soft-focus background-right with painterly atmospheric depth

═══════════════════════════════════════════
LIGHTING & MOOD
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Soft warm afternoon light, slightly diffused. Painterly warmth. The contrast between worried character's panic and Realistic's calm reads instantly — PIXAR storybook charm.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
TEXT RENDERING — PIXAR STORYBOOK TYPOGRAPHY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

This page has BOTH narration AND dialogue. Render with THREE distinct designed text elements:

1. **NARRATION PLATE** in the upper-left area (in the sky space):
   - Soft cream rectangular plate with hand-painted imperfect edges, painterly soft drop shadow
   - Korean text in warm sepia-brown hand-lettered designed typography:
     "누가 와서 울먹여요."

2. **SPEECH BUBBLE from the worried yellow character**:
   - HAND-DRAWN IMPERFECT speech bubble outline (slightly wobbly, charming — NOT a perfect digital oval). Soft white wash interior with subtle painterly texture
   - Soft drop shadow behind the bubble (looks painted, not digital)
   - Tail pointing down to the yellow character's mouth
   - Korean text inside in hand-lettered style with SHAKY/AGITATED feel (lettering itself slightly trembles to convey panic):
     "하늘이 무너지는 것 같아요!"
   - Maybe a small painterly squiggle or exclamation accent inside the bubble

3. **SPEECH BUBBLE from Mr. Realistic**:
   - HAND-DRAWN IMPERFECT speech bubble outline (same painterly style as bubble #2 for consistency)
   - Tail pointing down to Mr. Realistic's mouth
   - Korean text inside in CALM STEADY hand-lettering — visibly more composed than the worried character's lettering:
     "하늘은 멀쩡한데요. 무슨 일인지 차근차근 얘기해봐요."

Text styling:
- All Korean Hangul in warm sepia-brown ink (designed picture-book lettering, NOT system font)
- Bubbles feel hand-painted onto the page, integrated into the illustration

NO OTHER TEXT in the image — only the plate and two bubbles.
```
