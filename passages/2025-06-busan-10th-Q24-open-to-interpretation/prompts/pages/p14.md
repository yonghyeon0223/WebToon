## Character
- **Mr. Rational** — same as Page 12.

## Action
Mr. Rational holds up his brass balance scale at chest level. On the left pan: a small steaming bowl of 국밥 (Korean soup with rice). On the right pan: a small bowl of 샐러드 (salad with green leaves).
