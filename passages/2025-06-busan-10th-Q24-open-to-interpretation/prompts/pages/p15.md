# P15 — 화난 사람의 외침

## Character
- **Mr. Rational** — same as Page 12. Standing nearby, calmly observing — not yet responding.
- **Angry character** (cameo, debuts here): small round bright orange-red Mr. Men body, both fists clenched and raised, mouth wide open shouting, painterly steam puffs rising from the top of his head, red emotion-burst lines radiating outward from his body. Distinct silhouette.

## Action
The angry character mid-shout, fists raised at the sky in fury. Mr. Rational stands a step or two away, calm and still, balance scale at his side, observing without judgment. Body-language contrast: explosive heat vs cool composure.

## Background
**Village square** — same as Pages 4–5 (well, lollipop tree, surrounding houses). Late afternoon warm light. A few anonymous townspeople in the deep background going about their business, not central to the scene.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "누가 슬픈 일을 겪고 외쳐도 그는 같이 화내지 않아요." — plain sepia.
- Speech bubble (angry character, jagged aggressive lettering with thick wobbly outline): "너무 화가 나!" — plain sepia (cameo).

## Note
The visual joke is the warm/cool split — angry character is orange-red and steaming, Mr. Rational is steel-gray and still. Composition can subtly mirror this with warm lighting on the left vs cool blue-gray light on the right.
