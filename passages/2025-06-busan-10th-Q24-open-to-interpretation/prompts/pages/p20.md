# P20 — 둘의 외침

## Character
- **Baker** — same as Page 19. Mid-shout, even more animated than Page 19 — both fists raised, face flushed.
- **Florist** — same as Page 19. Mid-shout, mirror posture to Baker — both fists raised, leaning slightly forward, face equally flushed.

## Action
Both shop owners shouting the same words at the same moment, perfectly mirrored. Baker on the left, Florist on the right, both leaning toward the disputed patch of cobblestones between them, fists in the air. The mirror composition IS the visual joke — both equally certain it's THEIR spot.

## Background
**Bakery + Flower shop** — same as Page 19. Same midday warm light. The disputed patch of cobblestones at the bottom-center of the frame.

## Text
- Two parallel speech bubbles, mirrored composition (one tail pointing left to Baker, one tail pointing right to Florist), both rendered identically with jagged shouting lettering:
  - Speech bubble (Baker, top-left): "내 자리야!" — plain sepia (cameo).
  - Speech bubble (Florist, top-right): "내 자리야!" — plain sepia (cameo).

## Note
Both bubbles should look as identical as possible — same shape, same lettering, same size — to drive home the mirror joke. Position them symmetrically on opposite sides of the frame.
