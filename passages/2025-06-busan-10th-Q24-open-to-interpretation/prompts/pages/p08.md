# P08 — therefore 패턴 (vocab: therefore)

## Character
- **Mr. Logical** — same as Page 6.

## Action
Mr. Logical at chest-up to half-body framing, his slate raised with one mitten and the other hand pointing at it like a teacher demonstrating. The slate clearly shows "1.   2.   3.   ∴" in white chalk. A subtle painted arrow or visual cue leads from the slate up to the floating word *Therefore* in the upper area of the frame, tying the pattern visually to the word.

## Background
A scholarly warm-cream backdrop with soft-focus motifs — bookshelves, a chalkboard, ink pots — same general "Mr. Logical's world" feel as Page 6. Slightly more focused than Page 6 since this is an explainer beat.

## Text
- Narration plate (top or wrapping around the floating word): "뭐든 1단계, 2단계, 3단계로 이어붙이고, 마지막엔 꼭 **therefore**라는 말을 붙여요." — paint the English word "therefore" in cobalt-blue (Mr. Logical's signature word). Korean stays plain sepia.

## Vocab tag
- ***therefore*** — 그러므로, 따라서. 앞의 이유에서 결론으로 이어주는 말.

## Note
Compose with the bottom-right corner clear for the vocab parchment.
