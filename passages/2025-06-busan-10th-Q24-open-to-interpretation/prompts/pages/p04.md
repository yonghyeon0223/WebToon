# P04 — 흔들리지 않는 사람 (Mr. Realistic의 본성)

## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1. Walking calmly through the village square with magnifying glass raised at chest level, calm focused expression.
- 4–5 anonymous townsfolk (cameos) in various heightened emotional states: one wringing mittens worriedly, one waving arms excitedly, one bent close to another whispering a rumor behind a cupped mitten, one pacing with brow furrowed. Varied colors and shapes (round green, square pink, oval purple, butter-yellow, dusty-blue).

## Action
Mr. Realistic walks calmly through the village square, magnifying glass in hand, his pace slow and deliberate. Around him, the townsfolk are caught up in their feelings and rumors — animated postures, expressive gestures, subtle motion-line accents. He's the one still center in the swirl of activity.

## Background
**Village square** (debuts here): stone well in center, big lollipop tree, wooden bench to one side, surrounding village houses (Mr. Realistic's house from Page 1 may be visible among them for continuity). Warm afternoon light, same warm palette as Page 1.

## Text
- Narration plate (centered or top): "기분에 휩쓸리지 않고, 소문에 흔들리지 않고, 눈앞의 진짜 모습을 본다고요." — plain sepia.

## Note
This page visually establishes both the village square (which Page 5 will reuse) AND Mr. Realistic's defining trait — calm observation amid chaos.
