# P05 — 동네 사람들의 칭찬 (vocab: realistic)

## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1. Same calm half-smile, walking through the square or standing aside.
- 5–6 anonymous townspeople (cameos): varied colors and shapes (round green, square pink, oval purple, round butter-yellow, triangular sage-green, rectangular dusty-blue), each with a distinct personality and pose, gathered in two or three small clusters. (Different roster from Page 4; these ones are gossiping appreciatively rather than being chaotic.)

## Action
Mr. Realistic walks across the village square. The townspeople in clusters around him are talking ABOUT him (not to him) — leaning close to each other, glancing his way appreciatively, gossiping warmly. One prominent townsperson (the round butter-yellow one) leans close to a neighbor and whispers behind a mitten cupped beside the mouth.

## Background
**Village square** — same as Page 4. Same warm afternoon light, same composition anchor (well, lollipop tree, surrounding houses).

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "동네 사람들은 그를 두고 이렇게 말해요." — plain sepia.
- Speech bubble (the whispering townsperson, intimate gossipy lettering, small bubble with a small curved tail): "Mr. Realistic은 정말 그 이름값을 한다니까." — paint "Mr. Realistic" in his terracotta-orange.

## Vocab tag
- ***realistic*** — 현실적인. 자기 생각이 실제 현실과 일치한다고 믿는 상태.

## Note
First page where the word "realistic" earns its meaning — the townspeople validate that Mr. Realistic embodies it. Compose so the bottom-right corner is clear for the vocab parchment (no faces or key props there).
