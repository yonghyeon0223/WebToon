## Character
- **Mr. Realistic** — same as Page 1. Welcoming the visitors, calm host posture, magnifying glass on its cord at his chest.

- **Mr. Red** (main character — establishing reference for the whole book; must look distinctly different from Mr. Realistic / Logical / Rational AND from Mr. Blue):
  - **Body silhouette**: SHORT and WIDE — barrel-shaped. Slightly wider than tall (about 1.2× wide vs tall). Bottom-heavy / planted / stocky. Reads as solid, energetic, "ready to bounce up at any second."
  - **Color**: vivid vermilion red — saturated, slightly orange-warm. Distinctly more saturated than Mr. Realistic's earthy terracotta (which is a muted clay tone).
  - **Face features**: large round dot eyes — bigger than the three friends', expressive. Thick black eyebrows, often raised in passion / surprise / outrage. Wide mouth often open mid-cheer or mid-shout. Prominent rosy cheek-blush patches (red on red — slightly warmer red marks). Wears a **red baseball cap** with the brim facing forward, slightly tilted off-center for a casual fan look. A red bandana / scarf knotted around his neck.
  - **Signature props**: a small rolled red flag on a wooden stick held in one mitten (the kind sports fans wave). Sometimes a small red foam cheering hand instead.
  - **Posture & stance**: wide planted stance with both feet far apart; body slightly leaning forward; one mitten often raised holding the flag, the other gesturing. Reads as loud, animated, expressive — never still.
  - **Default expression**: passionate / animated / loud — the "loud sports fan" archetype.

- **Mr. Blue** (main character — establishing reference for the whole book; must look distinctly different from Mr. Realistic / Logical / Rational AND from Mr. Red):
  - **Body silhouette**: MEDIUM-TALL VERTICAL OVAL — egg-shaped, standing upright on its narrower end. Height ≈ 1.5× width. Top-heavy egg (slightly wider near the upper third, narrower toward the bottom). NOT round (vs Realistic's sphere), NOT a tall rectangle (vs Logical's slim rectangle), NOT a square (vs Rational), NOT a wide barrel (vs Red). A clear vertical egg/oval shape.
  - **Color**: deep royal blue — more saturated and slightly darker than Mr. Logical's cobalt-blue (the two blues must be visually distinguishable when seen side by side).
  - **Face features**: medium-sized round dot eyes (smaller than Red's, similar size to the three friends'), thin gracefully-arched eyebrows. Mouth in a focused intent expression — small open "go" mouth, calm but engaged. Subtle blue-tinted blush patches on cheeks. Wears a **blue knit beanie** (winter-style cap, snug fit) with a small white fabric pom-pom on top. A blue knitted scarf wrapped around his neck.
  - **Signature props**: a small rolled blue flag on a wooden stick held in one mitten (mirror of Mr. Red's red flag, but blue).
  - **Posture & stance**: upright, slightly leaning forward in concentration; weight more centered than Mr. Red's wide stance. One mitten often holds the flag, the other tucked or relaxed. Reads as focused, contained, quietly intense.
  - **Default expression**: focused intent / quietly passionate — the "focused fan" archetype.
