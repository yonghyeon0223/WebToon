# P07 — 비/우산/카페

## Character
- **Mr. Logical** — same as Page 6.

## Action
On a rainy street outside the cafe, pointing emphatically with one mitten toward the warmly glowing cafe entrance, the other hand holding his slate. **NO umbrella anywhere in frame** — the visual joke is that he deduced going inside instead of bringing one.

## Background
**Cafe** (debuts here): warm brown building with a cream/brown striped awning, glowing yellow window, a small chalkboard easel out front. Cool wet exterior contrasts with the warm yellow-amber glow spilling from the cafe windows. Light rain as delicate hand-painted streaks, wet cobblestones reflecting the cafe glow.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "Mr. Logical은 늘 이렇게 말해요." — paint "Mr. Logical" in cobalt-blue.
- Speech bubble (Mr. Logical, confident measured lettering, smooth bubble): "첫째로 — 비가 와. 둘째로 — 우산이 없어. **Therefore**, 카페로 들어가야 해." — paint "Therefore" in cobalt-blue, slightly bolder.

## Note
Don't include an umbrella anywhere in frame — that's the punchline.
