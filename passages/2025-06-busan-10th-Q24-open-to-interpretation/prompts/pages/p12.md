## Character
- **Mr. Rational** (main character — establishing reference for the whole book; must look distinctly different from Mr. Realistic AND Mr. Logical from silhouette alone):
  - **Body silhouette**: a rounded-square — almost a square but with softly rounded corners, like a die that's been sanded. Roughly equal height and width (1:1 ratio); the squat-square shape sits between Mr. Realistic's round sphere and Mr. Logical's tall rectangle. Centered weight (not top-heavy, not bottom-heavy). Reads as steady, methodical, "set in his ways."
  - **Color**: cool neutral steel-gray, solid (no gradient). Distinctly different temperature from Mr. Realistic's warm terracotta and from Mr. Logical's cool cobalt — sits as the most neutral / understated of the three.
  - **Face features**: medium-sized round dot eyes (slightly larger than Mr. Logical's, calmer / more open-looking than Mr. Realistic's). **Has eyebrows** like Mr. Logical, but a different shape — light gray slightly-sloped eyebrows that read as thoughtful / weighing (not Logical's confident arch). Mouth is a small composed thin closed-mouth line — slightly narrower than both Mr. Realistic's warm curve and Mr. Logical's measured smile (the most analytical of the three). NO blush — neutral cool face. NO head adornment, NO cap, NO hair (a clean rounded-square top — distinguishes him from Mr. Logical's mortarboard).
  - **Signature props**: a leather notebook held open in one mitten (lined ledger pages with two-column "장점 | 단점" headers visible), and a wooden pencil either resting on top of the notebook or held in his other mitten mid-stroke. A small brass balance scale (two pans on a brass beam) hangs at his side or from a belt loop.
  - **Posture & stance**: upright still stance with weight centered; mittens busy with notebook + pencil + scale. Body not tilted, not leaning. Reads as calm, analytical, methodical, even-keeled.
  - **Default expression**: small composed thin smile; calm calculating; reads "let me think it through."
