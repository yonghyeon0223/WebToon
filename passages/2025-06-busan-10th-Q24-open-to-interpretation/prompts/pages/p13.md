# P13 — 수첩과 장단점

## Character
- **Mr. Rational** — same as Page 12.

## Action
Mr. Rational seated at a small wooden table (or on a bench), notebook spread open in front of him, wooden pencil in his mitten writing on the page. The notebook is shown clearly enough that the reader can see four labeled columns: "장점 | 단점 | 비용 | 효과" — each with a few hand-drawn ledger marks underneath. His balance scale rests beside him on the table.

## Background
Warm scholarly setting — could be his desk, a quiet corner of the cafe, or a small study nook. A few stacked books and a wooden ruler in soft focus around him. Warm amber daylight.

## Text
- Narration plate (top): "Mr. Rational은 늘 작은 수첩을 들고 다녀요. 뭘 결정할 때마다 장점, 단점, 비용, 효과를 적어요." — paint "Mr. Rational" in steel-gray. The Korean phrase "장점, 단점, 비용, 효과" stays plain sepia (Korean rule).

## Note
This is a slightly longer narration; consider stacking it across two narration plates (top + bottom) if a single plate would crowd the frame. The notebook itself should be readable enough that the columns are visible — that's part of the storytelling.
